04

Ongoing Support

The brand doesn't stop at launch. This retainer keeps us in your corner for new pages, campaign creative, and whatever the next product moment requires. As StockIQ grows, the brand grows with it.

Deliverables: Monthly retainer · On-demand creative · Brand governance · New page builds

Platform Recommendation

Build it in Webflow.

Webflow delivers higher design fidelity without compromising development speed, critical for hitting your October deadline.

Its flexible CMS lets your team manage and scale content without heavy developer reliance. We're fully capable in WordPress, but for this project, Webflow is the faster, more agile choice.

Webflow
  • Faster to ship on deadline
  • Design fidelity preserved in-browser
  • Marketer-friendly CMS & visual edits
  • Native SEO hygiene & migration tools
  • Lower long-term dev maintenance
WordPress
  • Familiar, flexible ecosystem
  • Deep plugin + integration market
  • Higher build and maintenance overhead
  • Requires theme/plugin audit for speed
  • Backup option if needed
